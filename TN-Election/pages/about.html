About Us

Built to make TN election data easier to explore

bharathelection.com is a crowdsourced initiative that turns election information into clear, accessible visual stories for the people of Tamil Nadu.

We are sourcing election-related data and presenting it through structured pages, constituency-level views, candidate insights, and big-fight comparisons. This is an educational initiative by Gradious Technologies, with contributions from student teams and volunteers working to make public information easier to understand.

Who are we?

We are a group of contributors and volunteers who curate, validate, and organize election data from official public sources. Our goal is to present the information in a way that is easier to visualize and explore. We did a similar project for the Loksabha Election 2024 and it was a huge success. More than 1 million users were watching the live election result. Here is the link: results2024.in.

Are you official?

No. This website is not an official government or Election Commission platform. It is an independent educational project.

What are your sources?

We collect publicly available information from official Election Commission sources and related election data releases. We then review and structure that data for presentation on this site.

Why might the data differ from official sources?

Official sources may update at different times, and our team may need time to review and publish refreshed data. Because parts of the process are manual, temporary mismatches can happen.

Why are you building this?

We wanted to create a useful public-facing election experience that demonstrates how students and educators can build meaningful civic-tech tools with limited time and resources.
